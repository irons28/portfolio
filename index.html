About Me

I'm Ben, an aspiring data analyst and web developer based in South East England. After completing the Google Data Analytics Certificate and several coding courses through freeCodeCamp and Coursera, I've been combining my love for numbers with my passion for building digital things.

My goal is to create smart, user-friendly websites and data-driven tools - and eventually work independently, freelancing or building my own apps. I'm confident with tools like R, SQL, and Python, and I'm working on my front-end development skills using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me spending quality time with family, watching West Ham or planning my next learning milestone.
